### Compound Products / 复合食品

**EN:** For meals or products made from several ingredients, check the ingredient list carefully. If every ingredient is compatible and the product is not fermented or microbially matured, the product is usually compatible when sufficiently fresh.

**ZH:** 对于由多种成分组成的餐食或产品，应仔细查看配料表。如果所有成分都兼容，且产品没有发酵或微生物熟成，在足够新鲜时通常也可视为兼容。

**EN:** If not all ingredients are compatible, either treat the product as incompatible as a precaution or test personal tolerance carefully.

**ZH:** 如果并非所有成分都兼容，应谨慎地将该产品视为不兼容，或在个人耐受范围内小心测试。

**EN:** Additives can appear in basic foods where they are not expected; the ratings apply to pure foods without further additives.

**ZH:** 添加剂可能出现在看似基础的食物中；清单评级只适用于不含额外添加剂的纯食物。
